RAVENSHANE

Hello, I’m Raven I’m a new designer based out of Ottawa. I’ve created this website to give people some insite into who I am, what I do and why we should be friends. I was raised in a very rural area in Ontario by two creatives that nurtured my artistic side. When I graduated High School I moved to Ottawa and enrolled in a Music Production program. Although my love for music still prevails I realized while in this course that I was much more interested and passionate about visual art.

I learned how to paint and draw attending life drawing workshops and the occasional course at the Ottawa School of Art. I made a bit of money selling paintings, mostly to friends and family but spent much of my days and late nights working as a server and bartender. It wasn’t untill 2019 that I decided to take a leap and I enrolled in the Graphic Design Program at Algonquin College. I dove straight into the deep end of the world of design and have not looked back. Although my journey has just begun I’m excited to show you what I’ve learned so far.
